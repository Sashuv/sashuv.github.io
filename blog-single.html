What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment Analysis is a process of analyzing and measuring people’s opinion on various topics and statements. In other words, Sentiment Analysis means understanding and extracting feelings from data.

How did I made it?

For this project, I used Twitter. Twitter is a treasure trove of Sentiments. People all over the world share millions of opinions, reactions on every topic, every second. It’s like one of the biggest psychological databases which keeps updating from time to time.
So, to use the Twitter datas, I had access to Twitter’s API.
I used two libraries , Tweepy and Textblob.
Tweepy is a library for accessing Twitter's API and Textblob is for Natural Language Processing (NLP). With TextBlob, we get a polarity and a subjectivity metric. The polarity is the sentiment itself, ranging from a -1 to a +1, where 1 means positive statement and -1 means a negative statement.
And now I use all these results to find the sentiment of a data.
